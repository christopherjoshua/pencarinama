Escape Room - Bedroom

This game is inspired by escape games from MildEscape and neutralxe, and is in a way my love letter to the genre. It's not a big one, nor is it a complicated one, but I wanted to make games, and after mostly learning Unity by myself, I have created one that I hope people can enjoy as much as I enjoy making it.

WebGL builds are not supported on mobile devices.
